About Us

"In the bustling heart of Sta. Cruz, Marinduque, amidst the vibrant art scene and the comforting aroma of artisanal coffee shops, bloomed CarvedNature. The year was 2012, and a young couple, Gloria and Dioscoro, dreamt of a space that celebrated not just aesthetics, but also a connection to the natural world. Gloria, a sculptor with a passion for wood's inherent beauty, and Dioscoro, an eco-conscious entrepreneur, envisioned a company that brought handcrafted treasures straight from nature's embrace."


"CarvedNature's success is a beautiful example of what dedication and vision can achieve. They've become more than just a company selling things; they're storytellers. Their story celebrates nature's importance, taking discarded wood and crafting it into stunning, functional pieces. These timeless creations reconnect people with the natural world around them. Back in their Sta. Cruz, Marinduque workshop, Gloria and Dioscoro, lead a passionate team that curates a collection both useful and artistically crafted. CarvedNature serves as a constant reminder that the beauty of nature can effortlessly blend into our modern lives."
